# Chapter 2 Outline: The Jester in Knight Armor

**Arc:** 01 — Prelude to Chaos
**Status:** Outline Locked
**Outlined:** 2026-05-10

---

## Overview

Chapter 2 covers the pre-login phase: from Bima stalling Ferdi the same night Ch1 ends, through a 1–2 day gap, to Rara arriving with her own laptop and creating Aruna — ending the moment before she first enters the game.

Chapter 3 (First Login Chaos) handles everything after she logs in.

---

## Timeline

- **Night of Ch1** — Scene 1 happens. Bima stalls Ferdi via WhatsApp. Rara goes home. She figures out the name Aruna on her own, before she even has the game installed.
- **1–2 days later** — Scenes 2 through 6. Rara arrives with her laptop. Character creation through first login.

---

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1 — The Stall *(same night, Ch1 ending)*
Bima messages Ferdi via WhatsApp — not a cancellation, just a delay. Vague excuse: "ada urusan mendadak, bentar ya." Ferdi's reaction is comedically outsized for how little information he received. Rara is still in the room, watching Bima navigate this, enjoying it more than she lets on. No resolution — Ferdi is left hanging. The grand battle between Ferdi and Rara for the couple spot is banked for later.

**Note:** Keep Ferdi entirely on WhatsApp, not in-game. Establishes their real-world friendship beyond the guild. All-caps Ferdi on a phone screen is a different comic texture than guild chat.

### Scene 2 — The Laptop *(1–2 days later)*
Rara arrives at Bima's kos with her own laptop. Bima expects something mid-range. The specs are not mid-range — almost par with a gaming laptop. A beat of silence while he processes this. Then, possibly out loud: *"Mendingan aku aja yang pake."* The irony: Rara's machine is better, and Bima has been grinding for three years on the inferior one. This joke sets the power dynamic for everything that follows.

### Scene 3 — The Briefing
Bima goes into full tutorial mode: job classes, event mechanics, what "couple pair" means in practice. He is efficient about it. Rara listens in the way that looks like not-listening but absorbs everything in her own order. The gap between what he is explaining and what she is actually paying attention to is the scene's humor engine.

### Scene 4 — Character Creation + The Debate
The job selection screen. Rara lingers on Jester — the animations, the chaos energy. Bima shoots it down on practical grounds. She scrolls to Knight. She picks Knight not because Bima convinced her, but because the armor looks good on her character. Bima thinks he won the argument. He did not win the argument.

### Scene 5 — The Name
Rara selects male gender — deliberate, no hesitation — and types "Aruna." Bima notices. She explains the logic: Bentala was a strong, handsome name intended for a male character that ended up on a woman by accident. Aruna is the deliberate reverse — a soft, pretty name given to a male character on purpose. The couple pair now reads as a matched set. What Bima does not know: she figured this out last night, alone, before she had the game installed. She came back with the name already decided.

**Do not show Rara thinking through the name the night before.** Let it land cold. The absence of hesitation is the characterization — trust the image.

### Scene 6 — First Login
Rara's hands on her own keyboard. Loading screen. Bima watching from the side, no longer in control of the machine. Chapter closes before she moves — on the breath just before the chaos of Ch3 begins.
